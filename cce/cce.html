Team Guidelines

Communication

  • Clear comms
  • Target Focus
  • Pre-make plan A & B for next zone
  • Feed info to each other, steer each other

Confidence

  • You're all cracked, play with confidence
  • Trust that your teammates are there for you
  • Believe in yourself and your teammates

Awareness

  • Look around yourself at all times
  • Check for rats, run as a unit during rotations
  • Look for drones, especially from zone 3 and onwards

Breakdown Process

After each game: If we die, we stay silent for 30 seconds. Each person should analyze what they could have done better in that situation and return to the team with a fresh, calm mind.

We’re doing this to stop any negativity that could bring down morale when we play. If we die, it's not a big deal – we reset for the next game!

If you get knocked: Focus on helping your team. GIVE INFO! Do not sigh or scream in anger.
